# ⚡ AI-Driven Energy Intelligence for Educational Institutions

## 📖 Overview

AI-Driven Energy Intelligence for Educational Institutions is a data analytics and machine learning project designed to analyze electricity consumption patterns, generate predictive insights, and support intelligent energy management within educational institutions.

The project leverages data analytics, visualization, and predictive modeling techniques to help institutions monitor energy usage, identify trends, estimate future consumption, and improve operational efficiency.

This project was also published as a research paper in **IEEE GSEACT 2026**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze historical electricity consumption data.
* Identify energy usage patterns and trends.
* Generate predictive insights for future energy consumption.
* Support intelligent decision-making for energy optimization.
* Visualize energy metrics through dashboards and reports.

## 📊 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection

Collect electricity consumption data from institutional sources.

### 2. Data Cleaning

Remove inconsistencies, missing values, and duplicate records.

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Analyze usage patterns and identify trends.

### 4. Feature Engineering

Prepare meaningful features for model development.

### 5. Model Development

Build predictive models to estimate future energy consumption.

### 6. Visualization

Generate reports and dashboards for monitoring and analysis.

### 7. Insights & Recommendations

Provide actionable recommendations for energy optimization.
